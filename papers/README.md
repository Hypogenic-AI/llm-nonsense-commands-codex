# Downloaded Papers

1. [WordGame: Efficient & Effective LLM Jailbreak via Simultaneous Obfuscation in Query and Response](2405.14023_wordgame.pdf)  
   - Authors: Tianrong Zhang et al. (2024)  
   - arXiv: 2405.14023  
   - Relevance: Introduces dual obfuscation (query + response) to bypass safety; shows limits of alignment when prompts are disguised.

2. [FLRT: Fluent Student-Teacher Redteaming](2407.17447_fluent_student_teacher_redteaming.pdf)  
   - Authors: T. Ben Thompson, Michael Sklar (2024)  
   - arXiv: 2407.17447  
   - Relevance: Generates fluent, low-perplexity adversarial prompts to evade perplexity-based filters.

3. [Token-Level Adversarial Prompt Detection Based on Perplexity Measures and Contextual Information](2311.11509_token_level_adversarial_prompt_detection.pdf)  
   - Authors: Zhengmian Hu et al. (2023)  
   - arXiv: 2311.11509  
   - Relevance: Detection method leveraging token-level perplexity/graphical models to spot adversarial prompts.

4. [LatentBreak: Jailbreaking Large Language Models through Latent Space Feedback](2510.08604_latentbreak.pdf)  
   - Authors: Raffaele Mura et al. (2025)  
   - arXiv: 2510.08604  
   - Relevance: Low-perplexity jailbreak via latent-space synonym substitutions to evade perplexity filters.

5. [Great, Now Write an Article About That: The Crescendo Multi-Turn LLM Jailbreak Attack](2404.01833_crescendo_multiturn_jailbreak.pdf)  
   - Authors: Mark Russinovich, Ahmed Salem, Ronen Eldan (2024)  
   - arXiv: 2404.01833  
   - Relevance: Multi-turn benign-to-malicious escalation attack; highlights conversational vectors beyond single prompts.

6. [DrAttack: Prompt Decomposition and Reconstruction Makes Powerful LLMs Jailbreakers](2402.16914_drattack.pdf)  
   - Authors: Xirui Li et al. (2024)  
   - arXiv: 2402.16914

7. [AmpleGCG-Plus: A Strong Generative Model of Adversarial Suffixes to Jailbreak LLMs with Higher Success Rates in Fewer Attempts](2410.22143_amplegcg_plus.pdf)  
   - Authors: Vishal Kumar et al. (2024)
